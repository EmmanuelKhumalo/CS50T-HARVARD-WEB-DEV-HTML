RECORD STORES HISTORY



A record shop or record store is a retail outlet that trades recorded music.Garmophone records were available from the late 19th century to the early 20th century. More record formats were introduced during the 20th century including eight track tapes, compact cassettes and CDs.


In the present era record stores sell a variaty of items which include CDs, vinyl records and in some cases, they also sell DVDs of movies, TV shows, cartoons and concerts

The world's oldest record store is credited to Spillers Records in Cardiff, Wales. it was founded in 1894. initially it sold photographs, cylinders and shellac discs
