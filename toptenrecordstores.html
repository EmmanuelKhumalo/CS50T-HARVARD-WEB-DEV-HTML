TOP 10 RECORD STORES



The following is a list of the top 10 record stores around the world according to Marcus Barnes.


Marcus Barnes is an author, journalist, copywriter, DJ and overall music enthusiast.

The following content was extracted from the article "10 of the world’s greatest record stores – according to the author of a new book on the topic" written by Dan Stubbs on nme.com plus additional researched content on the subject.



1. 12 Tonar, Reykjavik, Iceland



Iceland is well known for producing very talented artists. This store is owned by some of these talented artists. The store is set up in such a way that customers can chill and have conversations with one another. The store overall is cosy enviroment to permit one to talk and socialise with friends and new musical enthusiasts


12 Tonar was established in 1998 in Iceland, its both a record store and a record label, since 2019 a cafe and a bar were added to the legendary store.



2. Alan’s Records, London



"Intimate and cosy, it’s a total sanctuary" Dan Stubbs writes. This is a record shop situated in London or accurately on East Finchley High Road


Alan's record and CD shop sell second hand records including CDs, Vinyl and tapes. They sell music from multiple genres with an enormous collection with over 15000 records, 3500 CDs and souvenirs.



3. Amoeba Music, Los Angeles, USA



One of the globe's largest record stores, you will find yourself setting up refuge there and spending a couple of days going through shelf after shelf of records; Hip-Hop, rock, classical, reggae and many more genres, the list is infinite. The origins of amoeba music chain began in 1990, it was founded by ex-employees of Rasputin records.


It now has locations in Berkley, San Francisco and Hollywood



4. Casarão do Vinil, Sao Paolo, Brazil



This is what every record fanatic dreams of, an entire chamber full of music. It’s not a normal house either, it’s a classic Brazilian mansion with plenty of its initial features still in place – It is worth visiting just for the aesthetic alone, even more so for the infinite selection of music on offer.



5. Happyfeet, Prague, Czech Republic



If you are a person who is unfamiliar with music from the czech republic, then this is the right place for you. The store is tiny and it is located inside a mall, you may oopt for second hand records or brand new ones. Genres range from rock, pop, soul, funk, oldschool, disco, Hip Hop and R&B and also local records from the czech republic.



6. Jazzhole, Lagos, Nigeria



As with many great record shops around the world, the Jazzhole in Nigeria's captial Lagos is not just a place to buy a record and leave, its more of a shrine for music lovers who acquaint with the tunes offered there. Kunle Tejuoso is the man responsible for this vibrant haven. He lets customers the chance to socialise and sit in the store, further more he lets local artist to perform there.



7. Mazeeka Samir Fouad, Cairo, Egypt



Operated by Ahmed Mohammed Ibrahim. A fresh well for a whole range of middle eastern music, it's a very hard shop to find, but the juice is worth the squueze once found. Despite the store selling a wide range of local records, you can also head home with a souvenir which may include but no limited to; a gramophone or an oud (local string instrument).



8. Pet Sounds, Stockholm, Sweden



A historical shop that has been around for many years, it is largely loved throughout the whole world. It is one of those shops which hold a very respected tradition, it's safe to say its one of those record stores which will be around for many more decades to come.



9. Rockers International, Kingston, Jamaica



It seems like this store is one of the last of a dying breed of vinyl trading in Jamaica, the aesthetics of the store resemble what seems to be more of a heritage site for physical vinyl sales. ‘Mitchie’ Williams is the emperor at the seat of this artistic shrine.



10. Sounds of the Universe, London



A record store connected to the label Soul Jazz Records.The store has a large collection of records ranging from Reggae, Dubstep, House, Disco, Funk, Soul, Brazilian, Latin and African. The store is constantly updated as there are regular shipments from USA, Jamaica, Japan, Brazil and Europe.
